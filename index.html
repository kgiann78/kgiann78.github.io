About Me

My name is Konstantinos and I am a Software Engineer, specialized in Service Oriented Desktop and Web applications.
My projects involve C4SRI and real/near-real time surveillance systems as well as implementing and REST and web services. Since 2014 I am a member of the Management of Data, Information and Knowledge Group (MADgIK) of the Department of Informatics and Telecommunications. I have been involved as a software engineer and associate researcher in the ATHENA Research Centre with two projects ESPAS and OpenMinTeD.
Currently I am a research associate under Prof. Manolis Koubarakis at the National and Kapodistrian University of Athens (UoA), focusing on Semantic Web Technologies and distributed queries on geospatial and temporal data. I am a member of the Knowledge Representation, Reasoning and Analytics (KRR&A) team, that belongs to the MADgik group.
